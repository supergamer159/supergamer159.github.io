HOW TO PLAY

BASICS
• Survive 100 waves — bosses every 5th wave
• Auto-shoot — focus on moving & dodging
• Collect XP orbs to level up & pick upgrades
• Kill combos boost your XP drops
• Beat all 100 waves to win
ENEMIES
• GRUNT / RUNNER / TANK — standard foes
• EXPLODER / BOMBER — stay away!
• NECROMANCER — heals nearby enemies
• TELEPORTER — blinks behind you
• JUGGERNAUT — charges at full speed
UPGRADES & CHESTS
• COMMON — minor stat boost
• RARE — strong improvement
• EPIC — build-defining effects
• Stats use diminishing returns — diversify!
• Boss chests offer 9 choices · Reroll costs gold
TIPS
• Spam SPACE — abilities have short cooldowns
• 🔥 RAMPAGE: 10 kills in 6s = 1.5× dmg for 8s
• 👑 ELITE enemies drop 2.5× XP
• Watch the minimap (top-right) for threats
• TAB to view full stats & items mid-run
